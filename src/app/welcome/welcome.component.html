About Us

Made by Sally is a home organization that specilizes in producing different types of cakes and sweets. Our concept is to provide clients with a home made flavour decorated with a professional and elegant design of your choice. In addition to the different types of cakes, we also provide a variety of delicacies such as decorated cookies, cup cakes, and several other products upon your request.



Our History

Our business started in 2015 and it was met with great success among all our clients. We managed to create a vareity of flavours and custom home made fillings that became very popular and were demanded frequently. Our designs and decorations were complemented occassionaly which was a reason of assurance to grow our business in order to reach more and more cake and sweets lovers.

If you are looking for a cake that serves both design and flavour, you've came to the right place, we care about our client's so much that we seek to provide the best experience possible.
